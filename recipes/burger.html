Burger Recipe

The perfect cookout (or in) with the Family

Ingredients Needed

  • Bun
  • White Cheese
  • Patty (beef)
  • lettuce
  • Spread

Cooking / Assembly Steps

Cooking

  1. prep the patty and preheat the oven to 475
  2. place the patty in the oven and let it cook
  3. take the patty out of the oven after 10 minutes

Assembly

  1. place patty on the bottom bun
  2. place the spreas on the top bun
  3. place lettuce and cheese on teh patty
  4. close the top patty on the burger
  5. Enjoy
